Ascension St. Vincent Evansville Breast Center

Mammography Waiting Area

Having a mammogram is a very personal matter. The waiting area was designed to feel comfortable and inviting. The space is calming and the furniture is elegant. I was commissioned by Jackson Begley Art Consultants. I designed the artwork collages from macro shots of nature. Soft and simple forms with a variant of colors and textures to compliment the space
