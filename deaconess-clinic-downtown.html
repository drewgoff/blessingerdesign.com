Deaconess Clinic Downtown Patient Corridors

Consulting with Artist Ann Parks

Ann is an amazing nationally known artist and photographer. Jackson Begley commissioned Ann for the Deaconess Downtown Clinic project. I had the privilege to work alongside both women in helping the project to fruition.

Wayﬁnding in hospitals and clinics can be routine, boring and understated. The requested approach from the architect was bold, colorful and dynamic. We needed an artist who could fulﬁll the requirements. Ann was a perfect ﬁt. The overstated artwork guides the patients to the correct exam room with elegance and a fresh nature-based approach. Ann’s photography are of ﬂowers native to Indiana.

Consulting was a privilege, and I am grateful to work with both amazing and inspiring women.

Corridors were photography by Alex Morgan Imaging.
