Koch Family Children’s Museum (cMoe)

Banners, Brand, Marketing & More

I believe that art heals. What better way to help a child discover and grow their imagination than through play.

In a culture saturated in virtual social interaction, cMoe delivers full sensory exploration that forge genuine human connections. I was glad to be an integral part of creating the cMoe brand and experience.

I served on the Board of Directors at cMoe and worked with Roto Studio on the initial exhibit designs, fabrication and ﬂoor layouts. We collaborated on everything and it was seamless when forming the brand to compliment the mission and space.

I designed all internal materials, signage, several additional gallery exhibits, all marketing materials, capital campaign materials, fabrication of custom dinosaur banks, and unique materials for special events.
