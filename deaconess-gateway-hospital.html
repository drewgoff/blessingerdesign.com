Deaconess Gateway Hospital

Central Lobby Art Installation

The Deaconess Gateway Hospital lobby is well known for its expansive open ﬂoor plan and unique curved walls.

I was commissioned to design custom art- work that would wrap this unique space. Designing for the area had it’s challenges. Not only were the walls curved ﬂoor to ceiling, they also had a support column in front of each areas.

Creating a visual centerpiece that spanned both curved walls while remaining unique allows the viewer to visualize the space as a whole, yet see each section individually. The artwork was installed in individual acrylic tiles that were thin enough to conform to the curved surface.

Color, shape and form carry the space and create visual exploration and interest.
