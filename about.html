Hello there!

I am so glad you’re here. I am designer and artist based in Evansville, Indiana. I have designed for a variety of companies for over 20 years. From corporate, health- care to non-proﬁt...I have designed for all types of industries.



I love to solve problems and explore possibilities to ﬁnd the perfect solution. Designing large format artwork for healthcare has been my passion for the past 15 years. I believe art heals. I also believe our surroundings profoundly inﬂuence how we think and feel. Collaborating with hospitals, architects and interior designers always enriches the outcome and ensures the artwork ﬁts the environment and needs of the client and patients.



Seven years ago, I transitioned from graphic design and creating large format artwork to UX/UI - Product Design. It was a leap of faith.



Product design and UX/UI has allowed me to problem solve in a diﬀerent way. Finding the simplest solution and under- standing core needs of a software application is rewarding. Storyboarding and persona research gave me a platform to think, design and grow software in an organic and granular way – similar to healthcare design, yet very different. It was a huge endeavor working directly with developers, but I love being able to collaborate and see software concepts made into fully functioning products. I truly love working digitally, everything from large format, brand identity, and product design. Turning form into function is the core of all three platforms.



When I’m not designing, you can ﬁnd me in the garden. Much of my artwork draws from nature and the simple beauty it provides.



I look forward to our next project together. Let’s make something beautiful.
