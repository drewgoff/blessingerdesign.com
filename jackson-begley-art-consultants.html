Jackson Begley Art Consultants

Brand Identity

Having worked with Chris Jackson, owner of Jackson Begley Art Consultants for over 10 years, I know how dynamic and vibrant her personality and company are. I also wanted to capture the rich legacy of Dr. Joseph and Gayle Begley, co-founders of Jackson Begley in the mark.

The brand had to be classic, regal and timeless. Jackson Begley has a myriad of clients and a rich history in our region.

Simple, strong yet elegant is the look and feel of the brand we created.
