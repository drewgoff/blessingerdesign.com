Evansville Regional Airport

Screening Area Art Installation

Evansville Regional Airport is a gateway to Southwest Indiana and is often the ﬁrst impression many visitors have when coming to the area.

The recent expansion and renovation project included custom artwork. The theme was the river and how it ﬂows through our area.

I took a modern approach with bold shapes representing the land and buildings with strong, white ﬂuid lines representing the river. The color palette compliments the bold blue accent color chosen for the gate entrance area.

The ﬁrst piece is 24 feet in length, ﬂoor to ceiling and ﬂows to the second piece which is over 17 feet in length and is a continuation of the river. The artwork was commissioned by Jackson Begley Art Consultants.
